    Creating Rules and Business Logic #

    From within an app detail screen, you can add rules and business logic. This is added via javascript.

    View App Business Logic #

    1. Select the app you want to edit in the application list.
    2. Click the Business Logic button. From here you can:
      • Create new rules
      • Edit existing rules

    Creating New Business Logic Rules #

    1. Open the business logic for an app (See View App Business Logic) and click the Business Logic button.
    2. Click the + CREATE button in the top-right corner of the screen.
    3. Complete the following details in the drawer that opens to the right and then click SAVE.
      • Hook Name
      • Hook Type
      • Operation Name
      • Code

    Editing Existing Rule #

    1. Open the business logic for an app (See View App Business Logic) and click the Business Logic button.
    2. Select the rule you want to edit.
    3. In the drawer that opens to the right, edit the hook details and then click SAVE.
